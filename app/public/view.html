What is KOC?

KOC is a virtual platform that allows you to compete again others in your city or others for dominance of your home town court or feild. The idea is simply to create an account sign into your "MyCourt" profile, find your local park and chat with others in the area and find a challenge. Once a challenge is accepted you play that group or individual in the sport chosen, winner is King of the Court, and the loser will have to try again another day.
